Description

Our AHA Rosaline Serum 10% gently exfoliates dead skin cells, unclogs pores, and boosts cell turnover for smoother, brighter skin. This lightweight formula absorbs quickly without stickiness, making it perfect for both day and night routines.

Benefits

  • Gentle Exfoliation

    Gently removes dead skin cells without physical scrubs, helping to smooth skin while minimizing irritation.

  • Brighter, More Even Skin

    Improves overall radiance and helps visibly reduce dullness and uneven skin tone.

  • Refines Skin Texture

    Enhances skin smoothness and clarity by refining uneven texture over time.

  • Lightweight & Fast-Absorbing

    Quickly absorbs into the skin with a lightweight, non-greasy finish.
